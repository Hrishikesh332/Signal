# Project Description

Market Signal is a live market intelligence platform that turns web research into actionable product and competitive insight. It combines a Flask backend, a Next.js frontend, and TinyFish-powered web automation to monitor market sources, collect structured evidence, and surface the results through a tactical dashboard.

The core idea is to give a team one place to understand what is happening in the market right now and what to do next. The platform tracks market and news sources, builds a live signal wire, supports competitor analysis from a company URL, and includes a product viability workflow where a user can describe an idea in natural language, optionally attach product images, and receive a researched commercial outlook. TinyFish acts as the primary live research engine, while OpenAI is used selectively for higher-level synthesis and ranking when needed.

In practice, Market Signal helps users answer questions like:

- What important market shifts are happening right now?
- Who are the strongest competitors around a company or product?
- Is a new product idea commercially viable based on live market evidence?
- What signals, risks, and validation steps should a team focus on next?

The result is a research and monitoring system that is faster than manual browsing, more structured than ad hoc notes, and better suited for decision-making than a raw feed of links.
